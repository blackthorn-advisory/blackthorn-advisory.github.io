We quote by case size and complexity. Most engagements use fixed-fee phases (triage → investigation → reporting) or hourly for open-ended work. Reach out to discuss your matter.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. Luxembourg roots, global work! We collaborate with counsel and partners across key jurisdictions.

Will you testify or produce court-ready materials?

We deliver court-ready exhibits and affidavits and can support counsel with declarations or expert testimony in select matters.
